Why Solartex

A Transparent Procurement Process

Open & Fair Competition

All tenders are open to any eligible registered business. Evaluation is conducted on merit — price, quality, and compliance with specifications.

Secure Document Submission

All supplier documents are handled with strict confidentiality. Only the evaluation committee accesses submitted materials.

Fast Evaluation Turnaround

Bid evaluations are completed within 14 working days of the tender closing date. Successful applicants are notified by official correspondence.

Dedicated Supplier Support

Questions about tender specifications? Our procurement desk is available Monday–Friday 8 AM–5 PM to assist prospective suppliers.
