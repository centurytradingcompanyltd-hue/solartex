Water Heater Supply

Ref: STG-009
Closes: To Be Announced
Category: Water Heating
Status: Open

Tender Overview

SOLARTEX GLOBAL COMPANY LTD invites sealed bids from qualified suppliers for the supply of electric storage tank water heaters and solar-assisted water heating systems. The equipment will be deployed across residential, hospitality, and institutional installations requiring dependable hot water supply.

Scope of Supply

  • Electric storage tank water heaters: 30L, 50L, 80L, and 150L capacities
  • Solar-assisted water heating systems with flat-plate or evacuated tube collectors
  • Insulated storage tanks (stainless steel or glass-lined)
  • Thermostatic controls and temperature/pressure relief valves
  • Mounting brackets, piping, and standard installation accessories
  • Installation and commissioning support included in scope
  • Backup electric immersion heater elements for solar units

Technical Specifications

  • Tank insulation: minimum 30mm polyurethane foam, heat loss ≤1.5 kWh/24hr
  • Heating element: 1.5 kW – 3.0 kW depending on tank size
  • Maximum working pressure: 8 bar with safety relief valve
  • Solar collector efficiency: ≥65% (flat-plate) or ≥70% (evacuated tube)
  • Compliance with IEC 60335-2-21 and applicable KEBS standards
  • Warranty: minimum 3 years on tank, 1 year on heating element

Eligibility Criteria

  • Certificate of Incorporation / Business Registration
  • Valid Tax Compliance Certificate (KRA)
  • Minimum 2 years' experience in water heating systems supply
  • Manufacturer authorisation letter (for distributors)
  • Audited financial statements for the last 2 financial years
  • Proof of at least two completed supply or installation projects

Required Documents

  • Completed tender application form (submit below)
  • Company profile
  • Registration and tax compliance certificates (certified copies)
  • Product datasheets and safety certification
  • Manufacturer's warranty terms

Evaluation Criteria

  • Technical compliance and energy efficiency — 35%
  • Price competitiveness — 35%
  • Supplier experience and warranty terms — 20%
  • Delivery and installation capability — 10%
