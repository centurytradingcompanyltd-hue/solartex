Shower Heater Supply

Ref: STG-007
Closes: To Be Announced
Category: Domestic Appliances
Status: Open

Tender Overview

SOLARTEX GLOBAL COMPANY LTD invites sealed bids from qualified suppliers for the supply of instant electric shower heaters and thermostatic shower units. The equipment will be deployed across residential developments, hospitality establishments, and institutional housing requiring reliable instant hot water at point of use.

Scope of Supply

  • Instant electric shower heaters: 3.5 kW, 4.5 kW, and 6.0 kW capacities
  • Thermostatic and pressure-balanced shower mixer units
  • Wall-mounted compact units suitable for retrofit installation
  • Safety cut-off and earth leakage protection components
  • Shower heads, hoses, and standard mounting hardware
  • Delivery to project sites with installation guidance documentation
  • Spare heating element kits for maintenance support

Technical Specifications

  • Power rating: 3.5 kW – 6.0 kW, single-phase 220-240V
  • IPX4 minimum water ingress protection rating
  • Earth leakage circuit breaker (ELCB) integrated or compatible
  • Compliance with IEC 60335-2-35 safety standard
  • Temperature control range: 35°C to 55°C
  • Warranty: minimum 1 year on heating element, 2 years on housing

Eligibility Criteria

  • Certificate of Incorporation / Business Registration
  • Valid Tax Compliance Certificate (KRA)
  • Minimum 2 years' experience in electrical appliance supply
  • Manufacturer authorisation letter (for distributors)
  • Audited financial statements for the last 2 financial years
  • Product safety certification (KEBS or equivalent)

Required Documents

  • Completed tender application form (submit below)
  • Company profile
  • Registration and tax compliance certificates (certified copies)
  • Product datasheets and safety certification
  • Manufacturer's warranty terms

Evaluation Criteria

  • Product safety compliance — 35%
  • Price competitiveness — 40%
  • Supplier experience and warranty terms — 15%
  • Delivery timelines — 10%
