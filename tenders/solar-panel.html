Solar Panel Supply

Ref: STG-005
Closes: 31 March 2026
Category: Renewable Energy
Status: Open

Tender Overview

Solartechglobal Company Ltd invites sealed bids from qualified suppliers for the procurement and delivery of photovoltaic solar panels and associated balance-of-system components. The equipment will be deployed across off-grid electrification projects, institutional rooftop installations, and hybrid solar-grid systems. Suppliers must provide panels from Tier 1 manufacturers with proven field performance records.

Scope of Supply

  • Monocrystalline PERC solar panels: 300 Wp, 400 Wp, 550 Wp, and 650 Wp
  • String inverters: 3 kW, 5 kW, 10 kW, 20 kW, and 50 kW (grid-tie and hybrid)
  • MPPT charge controllers for off-grid systems (12V/24V/48V bus)
  • Lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) battery banks: 5 kWh to 50 kWh
  • Aluminium mounting structures (rooftop pitched, flat roof, and ground-mount)
  • DC and AC cabling, combiner boxes, and protection devices
  • Delivery to project sites; installation and commissioning in scope

Technical Specifications

  • Panel efficiency: ≥21% (monocrystalline PERC) at STC
  • Temperature coefficient (Pmax): ≤-0.35% / °C
  • Module certification: IEC 61215, IEC 61730, MCS or equivalent
  • Inverter efficiency: ≥97.5% (Euro efficiency)
  • Battery cycle life: ≥3,000 cycles at 80% DoD
  • Performance warranty: 25 years linear power output guarantee (≥80% at year 25)
  • Product warranty: minimum 12 years on module, 5 years on inverter

Eligibility Criteria

  • Certificate of Incorporation / Business Registration
  • Valid Tax Compliance Certificate (KRA)
  • Energy Regulatory Commission (ERC) licence or exemption letter
  • Minimum 3 years' experience in solar PV supply or installation
  • Manufacturer authorisation (Tier 1 OEM letter of appointment)
  • Audited financial statements for the last 2 financial years
  • Proof of at least two completed solar PV projects of ≥50 kWp

Required Documents

  • Completed tender application form (submit below)
  • Manufacturer authorisation / dealership certificate
  • Panel and inverter datasheets with IEC test certificates
  • Sample system design (single-line diagram) for a 50 kWp installation
  • List of completed projects with system sizes and client contacts
  • ERC licence copy or exemption documentation
  • Bid security: KES 40,000

Evaluation Criteria

  • Technical compliance and product quality tier — 40%
  • Price competitiveness (equipment + installation) — 35%
  • Supplier experience and project portfolio — 15%
  • Warranty terms and after-sales support — 10%
